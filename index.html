About

Unified International Corporation is a network of private investors. Our Vancouver Based Capital company specializes in real estate. We use a proven methodology in order to leverage and raise capital to negotiate financing terms that satisfy both our clients and our investors. Our consultation rates are flexible in regards to payment options (form equity to the industry standard rates). Unified International Corp connects both debt and equity financing option to our A-Tier clients. Our ultimate objective is to ensure our clients with the best terms for their funding round. We help our clients transform their business to high growth enterprises with high multiple valuations. Our private network of high net-worth accredited investors move accordingly to the projects that are the right fit for their investment criteria.

Tech Development

Our partners have deep technical expertise in developing native mobile applications for both iOS and Android platforms. Our designers and developers are the best at what they do and can unleash the full potential of your app with each platform’s unique features. Sometimes it makes more sense to take the hybrid approach. Being able to write the majority of code for both operating systems with one code base is considered the holy grail of mobile.

We also build complex, dynamic websites that can be easily updated and maintained, allowing you to be self sufficient after we’ve worked our magic. We can deliver solutions using our own framework as well as on CMS platforms such as WordPress, Wix, and Squarespace. Whether you’re developing the website alongside a mobile app or enterprise software, we apply seamless integration across all platform and devices. A true responsive website cannot be an afterthought. We design and develop with cross functionality in mind so that user experience feels natural, and not forced to fit a mold.

Financial Services

Unified International Corp is a corporate finance services firm that helps growing companies access capital from a wide variety of institutional lenders, mezzanine funds, and equity firms. Public Transitioning: Transitioning companies from the private markets to public markets through companies that we’ve partnered up with. SR&E.D Tax Claims: Unified International Corporation partners up with a S.R&E.D specialist company to provide technology companies with the returns on their research and development claims. Capital Raising: Equity/Loan/Revenue Financing: Raising capital for projects in North America through financial institutions that we’ve partnered up with. A capital injection can range from equity, loan to revenue financing.
